About R. Moroccon Steam Bath Hamam & SPA

Located in the heart of Chuchchepati (Hyatt Gate Opposite), we bring authentic Moroccan and Turkish bath traditions to Nepal. Our facility features traditional hammam treatments, therapeutic steam rooms, and professional spa services.

We specialize in traditional body treatments, exfoliation, massage therapy, and wellness packages designed to rejuvenate both body and mind. Our experienced therapists are trained in authentic Middle Eastern and North African spa techniques.

Every visit to our hamam is a journey to wellness, combining the therapeutic benefits of steam therapy with the luxury of personalized care in an atmosphere of tranquility and cultural authenticity.
